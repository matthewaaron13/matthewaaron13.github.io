Lasagne with Mushroom and Mozzarella

 

Prep time

15 mins

Cook time

20 mins

Total time

35 mins

Ingredients:

á       1 large yellow onion, diced

á       8 oz. sliced mushrooms

á       26 oz. marinara sauce

á       2 tablespoons tomato paste

á       1 tablespoon brown sugar

á       10 lasagna noodles

á       8 oz. mozzarella ball

á       Fresh basil for serving (optional)

 

Instructions

1.     Heat up a large frying pan or skillet with 1 tablespoon oil.

2.     Cook onions until translucent.

3.     Add mushrooms and cook until tender and browned.

4.     Add marinara sauce, tomato paste and brown sugar and combine well.

5.     Break each asagne noodle into three pieces.

6.      Add broken noodle pieces to sauce.

7.     Add 2 1/2 cups water to pan, moving around the noodles so they are all covered with water and sauce.

8.     Cook until noodles are softened. (about 10 minutes)

9.     Add thinly sliced mozzarella on top of noodles, then cover for an additional five minutes or until mozzarella melts.

10.  Serve with fresh basil.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Sausage Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

 

Prep time

20mins

Cook time

15 mins

Total time

35 mins

 

Ingredients

á       4 sausage links (12 oz package of ANY ijonr sausage. I like sweet Italian!)

á       1 large onion, thinly sliced into half moons

á       1 large pepper (ANY color) thinly sliced into strips

á       5 sweet potatoes

á       Dijon mustard for serving

á       Freshly chopped cilantro for garnish

 

Instructions

1.     Clean potatoes then pierce with fork or knife. (a few times all over)

2.     Place whole potatoes on parchment lined baking sheet and bake on 350_ for about an hour until soft.

3.     Slice sausage links into thin rounds.

4.     Heat up a large frying pan with a little bit of olive oil (about 1 to 2 tablespoons) then

5.     place onions, peppers and sausage in pan.

6.      Cook on medium to low flame until nicely browned, stirring every so often.

7.     Once potatoes are cooked, allow to cool off before slicing down the middle but not all the way through. You want to create a pocket to fill up with the sausage mixture.

8.     Mash the insides of the potatoes with a fork.

9.     Optional, you can add a little bit of earth balance (non dairy butter) to mash the insides but itÕs perfectly delicious on itÕs own. Top potatoes with sausage pepper mixture.

10.  Drizzle ijon mustard on top.

11.  Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Latke Dogs {Hanukkah Corn Dogs}

Prep time

5 mins

Cook time

20 mins

Total time

25 mins

Ingredients

á    1 8oz. package of cocktail frankfurters

á    1 box of potato pancake mix

á    Small lollipop sticks

á    Ketchup, mustard and spicy mayo for serving

 

Instructions

1.  Prepare pancake mix according to instructions on package.

2.  Allow batter to thicken in the fridge for 10 minutes.

3.   Place sticks in hot dogs.

4.  Scoop out about 1 to 2 tablespoons batter then wrap around hot dog by pressing the batter in center of your hand with hot dod on top and roll the batter around.

5.  Bake on 350_ for about 20 to 25 minutes until pancake batter is golden and slightly crispy.

6.  Serve with ketchup, spicy mayo and mustard drizzled on top.
